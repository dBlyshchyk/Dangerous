Special Ops

Dangerous days out is an adventure company with a difference. We specialise in providing exhilarating adventures led by former British Special Forces operatives.

Mission Parameters

Choose to conduct daring missions, cross, deadly seas or become a covert agent in the field. We provide you with an exceptional task, the equipment and special forces expertise to enable you complete your mission

Derring Do!

Spy drones, attack dogs, reppelling operators and sophisticated secret agents all propel you into a world of espionage and intrigue conducting daring missions the require guts, guile and team work to succeed.

Corporate Packages

Dangerous Days Out can offer the perfect package for corporate organisations. Whether their on a mission to develop teamwork or the objective is a fundraising activity to boost their Corporate Social Responsibility policy. DDO has got your back.

Safety First!

We are fully insured and accredited to be able to offer you safe and effective adventurous days out.

  • SPECIAL
    FORCES RAID

    After careful reconnaissance of the enemy installation you rendezvous with the team to discuss the plan of attack.

    Carefully planted explosives, smoke screens, split-second timing and sheer guts will be essential to the success of this daring camp raid.

    Be mentored by former Special Forces operatives in how to conduct operations behind enemy lines and evade capture. Destroy an enemy base, rescue a captured agent and evade a hunter pack complete with dogs to be awarded your Dangerous Days Out tactical turtle neck.

  • Island
    Adventure

    Speeding across a slate grey sea and under the watchful eyes of your Special Forces instructor you receive the final coordinates to an enemy island.

    You kill the engine as you glide onto the beach, grappling hook at the ready. The stakes are high. Your mission: break into the fortified location, navigate man traps, destroy the enemy installation and get out alive.

    On completion of the mission be awarded a Dangerous Days Out Dagger.

  • Oxford
    Spy Craft

    Covert operators will instruct you in the dark and subtle art of spy-craft. Navigate the corridors of Oxford following cryptic clues whilst evading enemy agents.

    A cool head and ability to problem solve is a requirement of this unique and fascinating adventure. On completion participants can enjoy fine dining in a hall steeped in history.

MISSION EXTRAS

  • Have your adventure filmed in high definition by our award-winning videographer.
  • Evening guest speakers; adventurers handpicked by you to enhance your day. We can offer suitable evening entertainment and accommodation.
